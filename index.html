FAQs

Q: What are your rental rates?
A: Rental rates vary depending on the type of car, duration of rental, and any additional services you opt for.
Q: What is your cancellation policy?
A: We have a flexible cancellation policy that varies depending on the type of reservation. Please contact our staff for cancellation.
Q: How can I extend my rental period?
A: You can extend your rental period by contacting our customer service team. Additional charges may apply.
Q: What documents do I need to rent a car?
A: A form of identification such as a passport or ID card.
Q: Can I rent a car for someone else?
A: Yes, you can rent a car for someone else, provided the required documents are presented.
Q: Do you offer self drive services?
A: Presently, we do not offer self drive services.
Q: What happens if the car breaks down during my rental period?
A: In the event of a breakdown, our drivers are well enhanced to handle such issues.
Q: What is your fuel policy?
A: Our fuel policy depends on the type of rental agreement. You can opt in for self fueling.
Q: Can I take the rental car out of the country?
A: Our rental cars are permitted for travel within Nigeria.
Q: Are there any age restrictions for renting a car?
A: Yes, you must be at least 21 years old to rent a car from us. Additional age-related restrictions may apply for specific car categories.
Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept bank deposits and cash. Please do not pay online. Contact our staff for account details.
Q: Can I make changes to my reservation?
A: Yes, you can make changes to your reservation depending on availability and any applicable terms and conditions. Please contact our customer service team for assistance.
